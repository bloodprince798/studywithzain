Physics

Important questions and syllabus for Physics Class 9

20 Important Questions:

  • What is physics? Define its main branches.
  • Explain the concept of motion with examples.
  • Define force and explain Newton's laws of motion.
  • What is work, energy and power? Give formulas.
  • Explain the law of conservation of energy.
  • Define density and explain Archimedes principle.
  • What is pressure? Explain its types with examples.
  • Explain the structure of matter and states of matter.
  • Define heat and temperature, explain difference.
  • Explain the process of heat transfer.
  • What is light? Explain reflection and refraction.
  • Define sound and explain its characteristics.
  • Explain the working of simple machines.
  • What is electricity? Explain current and voltage.
  • Explain Ohm's law with circuit diagram.
  • Define magnetism and explain magnetic field.
  • What are conductors and insulators? Give examples.
  • Explain the structure of atom with diagram.
  • Define nuclear physics and its applications.
  • Explain the importance of physics in daily life.

Chemistry

Important questions and syllabus for Chemistry Class 9

20 Important Questions:

  • What is chemistry? Define its branches.
  • Explain the classification of matter.
  • Define atom and molecule with examples.
  • What is atomic structure? Explain with diagram.
  • Explain the periodic table and its importance.
  • Define chemical bonding and its types.
  • What are acids, bases and salts? Give examples.
  • Explain pH scale and its importance.
  • Define chemical reactions and their types.
  • Explain the law of conservation of mass.
  • What are organic and inorganic compounds?
  • Explain hydrocarbons and their classification.
  • Define metals and non-metals with properties.
  • What is corrosion? Explain prevention methods.
  • Explain the water purification process.
  • Define solutions and their types.
  • What is chemical equilibrium? Explain.
  • Explain environmental chemistry importance.
  • Define polymers and their uses in daily life.
  • Explain the importance of chemistry in industry.

Computer

Important questions and syllabus for Computer Class 9

20 Important Questions:

  • What is computer? Explain its characteristics.
  • Explain generations of computers.
  • Define hardware and software with examples.
  • What is operating system? Explain its functions.
  • Explain input and output devices.
  • Define memory and its types (RAM, ROM).
  • What is CPU? Explain its components.
  • Explain number systems (binary, decimal).
  • Define programming and programming languages.
  • What is algorithm? Explain with example.
  • Explain flowchart symbols and their uses.
  • Define data types and variables in programming.
  • What are control structures? Explain.
  • Explain loops in programming with examples.
  • Define functions and their importance.
  • What is database? Explain DBMS.
  • Explain computer networks and their types.
  • Define internet and its services.
  • What is computer security? Explain threats.
  • Explain the impact of computers on society.

Mathematics

Important questions and syllabus for Mathematics Class 9

20 Important Questions:

  • Define sets and explain operations on sets.
  • What is real number system? Explain.
  • Explain laws of exponents with examples.
  • Define polynomials and their types.
  • What is factorization? Explain methods.
  • Explain algebraic formulas and identities.
  • Define linear equations and their solutions.
  • What is coordinate geometry? Explain.
  • Explain distance formula and its applications.
  • Define geometry and basic geometrical concepts.
  • What are triangles? Explain types and properties.
  • Explain Pythagoras theorem with proof.
  • Define circles and their properties.
  • What is trigonometry? Explain ratios.
  • Explain mensuration formulas (area, volume).
  • Define statistics and data representation.
  • What is probability? Explain basic concepts.
  • Explain mathematical reasoning and proofs.
  • Define functions and their graphs.
  • Explain the importance of mathematics in daily life.

English

Important questions and syllabus for English Class 9

20 Important Questions:

  • Explain parts of speech with examples.
  • What are tenses? Explain all 12 tenses.
  • Define direct and indirect speech rules.
  • Explain active and passive voice conversion.
  • What are articles? Explain their usage.
  • Define prepositions and their types.
  • Explain conjunctions and their uses.
  • What are clauses? Explain types.
  • Define sentence structure and types.
  • Explain punctuation rules with examples.
  • What is comprehension? How to attempt it?
  • Define essay writing and its structure.
  • Explain letter writing (formal/informal).
  • What is story writing? Explain elements.
  • Define paragraph writing techniques.
  • Explain vocabulary building methods.
  • What are idioms and phrases? Explain.
  • Define synonyms and antonyms with examples.
  • Explain reading comprehension strategies.
  • What is grammar? Explain its importance.
